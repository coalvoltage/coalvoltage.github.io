About
This is the page for the class year 2017-2018 Micromouse competition. Micromouse is a robotics competition where students design small robots to solve a 16 by 16 cell maze. During this year, I competed with the team, Straight Outta Cheddar, in the UCR, UCLA, and UCSD 2018 Micrmouse competitions.
