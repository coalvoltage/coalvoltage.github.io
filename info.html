About Me

Hello, there! My name is Jimmy Im, an undergraduate at the University of California, Riverside who is pursuing a Batchelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering. I am enrolled in the UCR Honors program, hoping to improve my abilities as a student and advance my career. I am also a part of UCR's branch of the Institute of Electrical and Eletronical Engineers as an attendee of their general meetings and formed a team in their Micromouse competition, winning Best Beginner Mouse in Spring 2018.
